Hat care tips

Felt Hats

  • Dust regularly with a soft brush or damp towel in a circular, counterclockwise motion.
  • For deep stains, use a gum eraser or gently sand.
  • Handle by the brim, not the crown.
  • Never set your hat on its brim.
  • Avoid leaving in hot places.

Straw Hats

  • Wipe gently with warm, soapy water.
  • Rinse with a lightly damp cloth and air dry.
  • Avoid soaking to prevent damage.

About Us

Uncover Timeless Style
with Heritage Hats

Since 1983, Heritage Hats has kept alive the passion and traditions of the Southwest's cowboy lifestyle. Founder Rich Glisson learned the craft of hat restoration in Phoenix under the guidance of two mentors, working with original equipment dating back to 1912. What began as selling hats at local rodeos has grown into the largest hat store in Arizona.

Over 3,200 square feet of showroom space and an active restoration workshop in the back. Heritage Hats offers everything from classic western styles to panamas and fedoras, always with the same artisanal dedication that has earned the trust of customers including Ronald Reagan and George Burns.
