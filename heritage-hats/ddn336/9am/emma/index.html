Heritage Hats was founded with a passion for preserving the timeless art of hat-making. Each hat is carefully designed with premium materials, blending durability, comfort, and character. We believe a hat is more than just an accessory — it's a symbol of individuality and confidence. With every stitch, Heritage Hats carries forward a legacy of craftsmanship, creating pieces that can be worn with pride for years to come.

Most Common Questions

How do I know my hat size?

To determine your hat size, measure around your head about one inch above your ears and across your mid-forehead using a soft measuring tape. Match your measurement to the appropriate size on a hat size chart. If you need assistance, Heritage Hats offers custom fitting and shaping services to ensure the perfect fit.

Can I customize my hat?

Yes! Heritage Hats provides custom hat shaping and styling services. Whether you're looking to reshape a brim, change the crown, or add a unique band, their experienced staff can help you create a hat that fits your style and needs.

What are your hats made of?

Heritage Hats uses high-quality materials for all their hats. Felt hats offer a classic western look, straw hats are lightweight and breathable, and Panama hats are traditional, handwoven styles. They also offer a variety of other styles, including fedoras, flat caps, and outback hats, all crafted with durability and comfort in mind.

How should I care for my hat?

To keep your hat looking its best, brush it regularly with a soft-bristled brush or a damp cloth to remove dust. Store hats in a cool, dry place to prevent damage. Heritage Hats also offers professional cleaning, blocking, and restoration services to maintain your hat's shape and appearance over time.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, Heritage Hats offers international shipping. Please note that duties, taxes, or additional fees may apply for international shipments, as determined by your local customs office. Shipping fees are based on the total weight, quantity of hats, and the selected shipping method.
