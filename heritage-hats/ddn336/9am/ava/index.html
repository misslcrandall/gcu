Tips

Keep Your Hat Looking Its Best

A well-made hat deserves proper care. With the right attention, yours can last for generations.

  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Brush regularly to remove dust and maintain texture
  • Handle by the brim, not the crown, to preserve shape
  • Avoid moisture; if wet, let it air dry naturally
  • Use a hat box or stand for long-term storage

Keeping your hat timeless.
