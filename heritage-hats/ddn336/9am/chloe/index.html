Get to know us

The name Heritage Hats was chosen to reflect a deep respect for the traditions and craftsmanship of the American Southwest. After moving to Phoenix from Rockford, Illinois in 1981, the founder Rich Glisson became fascinated with western culture and was mentored by two expert hatters: Steve Speros of Phoenix Exclusive Hatters and George Falkedes of Athens Hats. They passed down the time-honored techniques of cleaning and blocking western hatsâ€”skills that are now central to the Heritage Hats identity.

Today, their legacy lives on through the continued use of original equipment dating back to 1912. Every hat is restored with the same precision, pride, and dedication that define true western craftsmanship.

Hat care tips

Use a soft-bristled brush to dust hats regularly. A damp terrycloth bath towel will also remove dust. Rub the surface of the hat in a circular, counterclockwise motion.

Services and events

Hats are an iconic part of Arizona's culture making them the ideal souvenir for your attendees. With over 30 years of experience, Heritage Hats began by outfitting local rodeos and quickly grew into a trusted event partner.

Whether you're hosting 25 or 1,000 attendees, we make the hat selection process smooth, professional, and stress-free. Partner with Heritage Hats and give your guests a functional, fashionable gift that represents the spirit of Arizona.
