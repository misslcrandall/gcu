Our History

Heritage Hats was founded from a deep appreciation for the traditions and craftsmanship of the Southwest, particularly the cowboy lifestyle. After moving to Phoenix from Illinois in 1981, I learned the art of hat restoration from two experienced mentors, eventually inheriting vintage equipment from 1912. My business began by selling hats from a pickup truck at rodeos, and eventually in 1983, the first store opened.

Yours truly, Rich Glisson

Today, Heritage Hats is Arizona's largest hat retailer, offering a wide range of styles and maintaining an on-site workshop for cleaning, blocking, and custom hat-making. Notable clients have included Ronald Reagan and George Burns. Visitors can explore the store online through videos and virtual tours.

Hat Care Advice

Buying a hat is an investment, so its important to keep your apparel choice at its finest.

Materials

-Soft bristled brushes

-Terrycloth bath towel

-Gum eraser

-Synthetic soap or detergent

Specific Accommodations

For Felt Hats

Use a gum eraser for deep stain cleaning!

*Never set your hat down by the brim

Prevent hat from resting in hot areas to avoid damage

For Straw Hats

Wash with synthetic detergent or soap using a soft cloth

*Be aware that too much water causes damage!
