About Heritage Hats

Since 1983, Heritage Hats has been Arizona's largest hat store, offering western, fedora, Panama, Australian, and more. What started as selling hats from a pickup at rodeos grew into a 3,200 sq. ft. shop with two showrooms and an on-site restoration workshop.

We honor time-tested craftsmanship with original 1912 equipment, custom hat-making, and expert cleaning and blocking — serving everyone from local cowboys to legends like Ronald Reagan and George Burns.

Rich Glisson, Owner
