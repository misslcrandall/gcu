About us

Heritage Hats began with my love for the cowboy way of life after moving to Phoenix in 1981. Mentored by master hatters, I learned the craft of cleaning, blocking, and restoring hats using equipment from 1912.

What started with selling hats from my pickup grew into Arizona’s largest hat store, offering western hats, fedoras, panamas, Australians, and more. In our workshop, we restore and custom-make hats—serving everyone from locals to legends like Ronald Reagan.

— Rich Glisson, Owner
