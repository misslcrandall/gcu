OUR STORY

Founded from a love for Southwest tradition, Heritage Hats began when Rich Glisson moved to Phoenix in 1981 and learned hat restoration from master hatters Steve Speros and George Falkedes. With vintage equipment from 1912, Rich started selling hats at rodeos before opening his first store in 1983.
