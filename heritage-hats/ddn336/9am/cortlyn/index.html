UNDER THE BRIMS

Heritage Hats was founded from a passion to preserve the traditions of the Southwest. After moving from Illinois to Phoenix in 1981, the founder fell in love with cowboy life and learned hat restoration from mentors Steve Speros and George Falkedes, inheriting their 1912 equipment.

Starting from selling hats at rodeos, the first store opened in 1983. Today, Heritage Hats is Arizona’s largest hat store, offering western, fedora, panama, Australian styles, and custom hats. Our in-house workshop provides expert cleaning and blocking.

Recognized by Arizona Highways, the Arizona Republic, and Phoenix Magazine, we’ve proudly served customers including Ronald Reagan and George Burns.
