Hat Care Tips

General Tips

At Heritage Hats, we know buying a hat is an investment...

For Felt Hats

  • Do not handle the crown directly.
  • Do not set your hat down on the brim.
  • Keep away from heat.

For Straw Hats

  • Wipe with a soft cloth dipped in mild soap solution.
  • Rinse and air dry.
  • Do not soak the hat.
