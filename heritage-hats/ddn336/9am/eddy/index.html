About Us

Welcome to the biggest hat retailer in Arizona, Heritage Hats! For residents, visitors, as well as hat enthusiasts across the country, we provide hats that are both custom-made or branded. In addition to hat bands and accessories to customize your style, we have westerns, fedoras, Panamas, Australians, Kangols, pig pies, derbies, top hats, and flat caps. To keep your favorites in great condition, we also provide hat restoration, cleaning, and blocking services. Many designs provide very comfortable solutions for patients receiving radiation or chemotherapy, as well as UPF 50+ sun protection. At Heritage Hats, we think that hats combine protection, style, along with tradition. To locate the ideal hat for you, stop by our Phoenix location or browse our website.
