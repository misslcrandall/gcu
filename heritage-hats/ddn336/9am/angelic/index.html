About Us

Located in Phoenix, Arizona, we are proud to be the largest hat store in the state,
offering an unmatched selection of quality headwear and expert services since 1983.
We offer one of the most extensive collections of hats in the Southwest. From classic
western styles and fedoras to Panama hats, Australian styles, novelty hats, and more.
We stock a wide range of sizes and styles to fit every head and every personality.

Our Services

Cleaning

We offer dry cleaning for your hat. We also handle felt hats such as western, dress, and fedoras for cleaning & blocking. However, we do not offer cleaning/blocking for canvas or straw hats.

Blocking

Re-blocking and shaped to the style you desire, and replacing your sweatband if necessary. There are separate charges for re-shaping crown or re-shaping brim.

Restoration

Replace your sweatbands, linings, and making style changes if desired. Restoration services are suited for older hats or ones in rougher shape. We restore them so that they look newer or closer to original shape/quality.

Customs

If you want a hat made with certain specs or special orders such as size, shape, brim, etc., we can do that!

Our Styles

Fedora Felt & Straw

Classic fedoras have a pinched front, snap brims, dress-look, etc. Our felt fedoras are more formal/traditional. Straw styles are lighter for warm weather.

Western Felt & Straw

Western felt and straw hats are breathable and rugged, ideal for outdoor, work, or ranch settings.

Panama

Dressy, lightweight, breathable hats perfect for summer or formal wear.

Australian

Felt or weather-resistant materials, wide brims, rugged design for sun and rain protection.
