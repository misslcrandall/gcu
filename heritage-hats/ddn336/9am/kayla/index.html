ABOUT THE
BRAND

Heritage Hats was founded from a passion to preserve the western tradition of hat making. After moving to Phoenix in 1981, I learned the craft of cleaning and blocking hats from mentors Steve Speros and George Falkedes, inheriting their original 1912 equipment. I began selling hats from my truck and opened my first store in 1983. Today, Heritage Hats is Arizona's largest hat store, offering a wide variety of western, fedora, Panama, and Australian styles, along with custom creations. Our workshop continues the tradition of on-site cleaning, blocking, and custom work, serving customers ranging from local cowboys to icons like Ronald Reagan and George Burns.

-Rich Glisson, Heritage Hats Owner
