Our Story

For over 40 years, Heritage Hats has been committed to keeping Arizona covered with our wide range of high-quality hats. Founded in the soul of the Southwest,our company was built on the idea that a hat is more than an accessory. It is a symbol of pride, hard work, resilience, and heritage.

Since the 1980s Heritage Hats has grown but our commitment to our roots stays the same. We strive to preserve the western culture of the Southwest while serving generations of hardworking people who value quality and authenticity.
